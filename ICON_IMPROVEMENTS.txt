💡 BEST PRACTICES

1. ALWAYS USE:
   ✅ Appropriate size classes
   ✅ Consistent colors
   ✅ Hover effects
   ✅ Tooltips on icon-only buttons
   ✅ Proper contrast

2. NEVER USE:
   ❌ Inline sizes (use classes)
   ❌ Low contrast combinations
   ❌ Icons without context
   ❌ Mixed sizing in same context

3. CHECK:
   ✅ Icon is clearly visible
   ✅ Color matches context
   ✅ Size is appropriate
   ✅ Hover state works
   ✅ Accessible (has label/title)
